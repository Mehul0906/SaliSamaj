Our History

Swakul Sali Seva Samaj, Nashik Road was established in 19. Initially started by a few socially conscious individuals on a small scale, the organization has grown over the years. Today, our society has its own building where various social and cultural programs are organized.

Our Objectives

  • To foster unity among all sections of society
  • To preserve and protect cultural heritage
  • To help the needy and underprivileged in society
  • To provide educational guidance and skill development programs for youth
  • To implement various initiatives for women's empowerment

Our Working Methodology

Our society organizes an annual general meeting where all members come together to review the year's activities and plan for the coming year. An executive committee is elected to oversee the daily functioning of the organization.
